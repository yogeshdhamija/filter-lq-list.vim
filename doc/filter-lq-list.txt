*filter-lq-list.txt*

Author: Yogesh Dhamija <yogeshdhamija@outlook.com>

This plugin allows the |location-list| and the |quickfix| list to be filtered.
You'll be able to delete lines there using `dd` or `dj` or `Vd` or similar,
and it'll remove those lines from the list.

`d` is a real |operator| here, so counts and motions work as they do anywhere
else: `dd`, `5dd`, `d5j`, `dG`, `dap`, `Vjjjd`.

The entries themselves are never re-parsed from the text on screen, so they
survive filtering intact -- the list stays navigable, and 'quickfixtextfunc'
is left alone. The list is filtered in place; use |:colder| to reach lists
from before it was built, not to undo a filter.
